Our Company

Founded By Fans,
For Fans Who Share Our Passion

At Team Double Dragon, we create because of what we love. Born from a shared passion for anime, gaming, and creativity, our mission is simple: to build a platform that celebrates fandom and connects fans worldwide. We’re not in it for corporate greed; we’re here to bring joy, inspire imagination, and foster a vibrant community of like-minded enthusiasts.

Our Values

Foundations Of team Double Dragon

Passion For Fandom

We understand that anime, gaming, and all forms of creative expression bring joy and inspiration to countless fans. Our mission is to create experiences that resonate deeply with our community, celebrating the vibrant worlds that unite us.

Progress Through Collaboration

We believe in the power of collaboration to uplift and inspire one another. At D2X, we are committed to empowering fans and creators alike, ensuring that everyone has a voice in shaping our platform.

Community First

D2X is dedicated to fostering an inclusive environment where fans can connect, collaborate, and share their love for their favorite genres. By prioritizing the voices of our community, we aim to build a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.
